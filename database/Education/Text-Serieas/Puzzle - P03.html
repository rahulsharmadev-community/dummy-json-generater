Qus : 1
Who among following is 24 yr old?
  1. E
  2. A
  3. H
  4. G
  5. B
View Passage View Solution
There are eight persons A, B, C, D, E, F, G and H are sitting around a circular table. All are facing towards the center of the table. All of them has different age viz. 19 yr, 20 yr, 21 yr, 22 yr, 23 yr, 24 yr, 25 yr and 26 yr but not necessarily in the same order.
G sits third to left of B who is 21 yr old. Three persons sit between G and C who is 19 yr old. Two persons sit between C and H. The one who is 26 yr old sits immediate left of H. Two persons sit between the one who is 26 yr old and the F who is 20 yr old. A faces E but none of them is 26 yr old. D does not sit adjacent to E. The one who is 25 yr old sits third to right of one who is 22 yr old. The one who is 24 yr old sits immediate left of one who is 25 yr old.

Qus : 6
Who among following sits immediate left of F ?
  1. No one
  2. C
  3. D
  4. B
  5. E
View Passage View Solution
Eight friends A, B, C, D, E, F, G and H are seated in a straight line at an equal distance between each other, but not necessarily in the same order. Some of them are facing north and some are facing south. 
Three persons sit between B and E. H sits second to right of B. H sits one of the end of line. C sits third to left of G. Neither C nor G sits adjacent to E. A sits second to left of G. D does not sit any of the extreme end of the line. Immediate neighbour of G faces opposite direction (i.e If one neighbour of G faces north then other faces south and vice-versa) . The one who sits at the extreme end faces opposite direction (i.e If one of the person who sits one of the extreme end faces north then other person who sits other end faces south and vice-versa). C and D faces same direction as G ( Same direction means if G faces north then both C and D faces north and vice-versa). E sits immediate left of D and both of them faces north direction. Immediate neighbour of B faces opposite direction (i.e If one neighbour of B faces north then other faces south and vice-versa).
